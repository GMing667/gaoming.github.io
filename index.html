Ming Gao is currently a Professor of the School of Computer Science at Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications. He obtained the Ph.D. degree from the school of cyber science and technology, Zhejiang University (ZJU) in 2024, advised by Prof. Jinsong Han and co-advised by Prof. Feng Lin. He received his bachelor degree in Electrical Engineering and Automation and master degree in Computer Science from Xi'an Jiaotong University (XJTU) in 2018 and 2021 respectively.

His research interests include cyber-physical security, mobile computing, and privacy protection.
