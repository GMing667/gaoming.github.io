He is a Ph.D. student at the school of cyber science and technology, Zhejiang University (ZJU), under the supervision of Prof. Jinsong Han. He received his B.Eng in Electrical Engineering and Automation and M.Sc in Computer Science from Xi'an Jiaotong University (XJTU).

His research interests include cyber-physical security, mobile computing, and privacy protection.
