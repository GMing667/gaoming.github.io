He is a Ph.D. student at the school of cyber science and technology, Zhejiang University (ZJU), under the supervision of Prof. Jinsong Han and Prof. Feng Lin. He received his bachelor degree in Electrical Engineering and Automation and master degree in Computer Science from Xi'an Jiaotong University (XJTU).

His research interests include cyber-physical security, mobile computing, and privacy protection.
